Welcome to my website!

I am a student at KEA and a software developer in the making.
I love creating things with my hands, making silly gifs, early internet aesthetic and html.

In collaboration with SOFTER, my friend Cecil and I have a monthly queer coding club, where we create fun things with code and hone our skills with other sweet, nerdy queers. We meet every month on the last Sunday at the SOFTER LAB in Østerbro, and this website is the first of many projects I will work on along the way.

It is a mix of some the things I like, make, think as well as a little playground for future knowledge. Hope you have fun visiting!

If you'd like to get in touch, to join the coding club or want to pitch a cool project to me, you can write me an email.

If you want to know more about my work find me on LinkedIn, or check out my GitHub.

This website is inspired by the impeccable aesthetic of other html enthusiasts on the web.

Thank you for the inspiration to be a better programmer and have fun with it ♥
